We’d love to work alongside you! Even if you only have an hour or two a week to volunteer, we need your support to make municipal broadband a reality in Portland.

We’re especially looking for people who have experience with any of the following:

  • Drafting public policy
  • Administering 501(c)(4) organizations
  • Marketing and advertising

Visit Our Chat

{% include icons/icon-chat.svg %} irc.freenode.net #pdxfiber

The easiest way to get in touch with us is via chat. We have an active IRC community on the freenode network. Let us know what you’re interested in working on, and we’ll get you involved right away!

Come to a Meeting

We post all our upcoming meetings to our Meetup group. We love seeing new faces, and we make a point to be a welcoming, inclusive community for everyone.

We’re Open Source

{% include icons/icon-github.svg %} GitHub

Found a problem with our website? Want to expand a page based on your research? Open an issue or pull request on our GitHub repository.

If you’re not in the Portland area, but are interested in starting a municipal broadband campaign in your community, feel free to fork and modify our website to your needs. Our website is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International license. We’d love to help you get started, so please also reach out via IRC or email!
